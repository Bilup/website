## Personalize Your Editor

Give your Scratch editor a unique look with our powerful theme editor. Custom Theme allows you to fully control the visual style of your workspace — from subtle refinements to bold brand identities.

### Theme Customization

#### Gradient Themes
- Create smooth multi-color gradient interfaces
- Adjust color stops and transitions
- Build modern and visually rich editor styles
- Perfect for dynamic and creative designs

#### Solid Color Themes
- Apply clean, single-color themes
- Maintain a minimal and focused workspace
- Match your organization or brand colors
- Ensure visual consistency across projects

### Theme Management

#### Import Themes
- Load previously saved theme files
- Share themes with your team or community
- Quickly switch between different styles
- Reuse themes across multiple projects

#### Export Themes
- Save your custom themes as files
- Back up your preferred configurations
- Distribute branded themes easily
- Maintain consistency across devices

Our Custom Theme system is designed to make personalization simple, flexible, and powerful — helping you create an editor experience that truly matches your style.
